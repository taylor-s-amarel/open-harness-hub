# Google Gemini (multimodal frontier)

*adapter* · `adapter/google-gemini` · v0.1.0 · stable

Google Gemini frontier-class adapter — strongest of the three
big-three frontier vendors on multimodal (vision + audio + video)
tasks. Use when the pipeline needs to process images / video as
primary inputs (radiology, satellite imagery for deforestation
monitoring, audio grievance recordings).

Requires `GOOGLE_API_KEY` (or workload-identity in GCP).
