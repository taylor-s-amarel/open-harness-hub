# ESG environmental (E) sub-rubric v1

*rubric* · `rubric/esg-env-v1` · v0.1.0 · experimental

Five-dimension rubric for grading ONLY the environmental dimension
of a supplier disclosure. Aligned to CSRD ESRS E1-E5, EUDR, and
CSDDD Art. 13 (climate transition plan).

Use this when you want focused E-grading; use
`rubric/esg-supplier-compliance-v1` for combined E+S+G grading.
