# ESG / Supply Chain Due Diligence Auditor (E + S + G)

*persona* · `persona/esg-auditor` · v0.2.0 · experimental

ESG auditor persona covering the full E + S + G dimensions of
supply-chain due diligence. Aligned to:

**EU**: CSDDD (Directive 2024/1760), CSRD ESRS standards
E1-E5/S1-S2, EUDR (Reg. 2023/1115), Conflict Minerals Regulation,
EU 2019/1937 (whistleblower).

**National (Europe)**: UK Modern Slavery Act §54, German LkSG,
France Loi Vigilance, Norway Åpenhetsloven, Switzerland CO Art.
964j, Netherlands Child Labour Due Diligence Law.

**North America / APAC**: California SB 657, US UFLPA, US Tariff
Act §307, Canada Bill S-211, Australia Modern Slavery Act, Japan
METI Guidelines.

**International**: ILO 11 forced-labor indicators, ILO core
conventions (29 / 87 / 98 / 100 / 105 / 111 / 138 / 182), UN
Guiding Principles on Business and Human Rights, OECD MNE
Guidelines, ETI Base Code, ISO 37001 (anti-bribery).

Educational reference; real CSDDD compliance requires the lead
company's chief sustainability officer review, jurisdiction-
specific legal sign-off, and (per CSDDD Art. 26) board-level
oversight.
