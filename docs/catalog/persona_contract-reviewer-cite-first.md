*persona* · `persona/contract-reviewer-cite-first` · v0.1.0 · experimental

NDAs, MSAs, SOWs, vendor agreements, employment contracts, license
agreements. Citation-first: every red-flag refers to the
knowledge-pack/contract-law-clauses entry that grounds the
recommendation. Educational reference — does not substitute for
external counsel on regulated matters.
