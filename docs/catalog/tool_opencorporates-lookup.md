# OpenCorporates company lookup

*tool* · `tool/opencorporates-lookup` · v0.1.0 · stable

Look up a company in the OpenCorporates global registry (220M+
legal entities). Returns: jurisdiction code, incorporation date,
company status, registered address, officers, beneficial-ownership
filings where disclosed. Used by ESG / KYC / vendor-onboarding
pipelines to verify a counterparty is a real legal entity.

Free tier: 50 queries/day. Requires OPENCORPORATES_API_KEY for
higher tiers.
