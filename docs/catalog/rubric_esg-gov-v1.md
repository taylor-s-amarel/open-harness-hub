# ESG governance (G) sub-rubric v1

*rubric* · `rubric/esg-gov-v1` · v0.1.0 · experimental

Six-dimension rubric for grading ONLY the governance dimension of
a supplier disclosure. Aligned to OECD MNE Guidelines (Chapter II
general policies + Chapter VII anti-bribery), UK Bribery Act 2010,
US FCPA, EU 2019/1937 (whistleblower), and ISO 37001.

Use this when you want focused G-grading; use
`rubric/esg-supplier-compliance-v1` for combined E+S+G grading.
