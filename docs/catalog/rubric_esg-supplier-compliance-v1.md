# ESG supplier compliance v1 (full E + S + G)

*rubric* · `rubric/esg-supplier-compliance-v1` · v0.2.0 · experimental

Twelve-dimension rubric for grading supplier-submitted policy texts,
self-assessments, and grievance transcripts against the CSDDD, the
ILO forced-labor indicators, the major national supply-chain laws,
CSRD ESRS environmental disclosures, and the lead company's code
of conduct. Separates the **E**, **S**, and **G** dimensions
explicitly so a supplier can score high on one and be flagged on
another.

Used by `pipeline/supplier-policy-grading` and `pipeline/deep-tier-
supplier-audit`. For tighter sub-domain grading use the
per-dimension rubrics `rubric/esg-env-v1`, `rubric/esg-social-v1`,
`rubric/esg-gov-v1`.
