# Step-Back Prompting

*pattern* · `pattern/step-back-prompting` · v0.1.0 · stable

Before answering, the model is prompted to derive a MORE ABSTRACT
question that captures the essence of the user's question. Retrieval
and reasoning then happen against the abstract question; the model
applies the resulting principle to the specific case. Strong for
reasoning tasks where the surface form of the question obscures the
underlying concept.
